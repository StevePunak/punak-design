What We Do

Services

We design and build the software that operators, technicians, and engineers rely on to monitor and control complex systems.

Control Display Applications

Full-featured GUI applications for device configuration, monitoring, and control. Multi-document interfaces, real-time data visualization, and operator-friendly workflows.

Protocol Integration

Bridging your display to the hardware. CAN bus, Modbus, MQTT, serial, TCP/IP, HTTP REST APIs — whatever your devices speak, we connect it.

Embedded HMI

Touch-screen interfaces for embedded Linux and RTOS platforms. Optimized for the constrained environments where your product actually runs.

Engineering & Manufacturing Tools

Developer utilities, factory test stations, calibration tools, and diagnostics applications that your engineering team needs to build and support your product.

Simulation & Test

Hardware simulators and automated test harnesses so you can develop and validate software before the hardware is ready.

Systems Architecture

End-to-end design of the software systems that tie your devices, cloud, and operator interfaces together. Requirements through deployment.

Who We Work With
